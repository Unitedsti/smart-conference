Title Sustainable supply chains in the Big Data-Industry 4.0 era: a Balanced Scoredcard Approach.
Paper (Mar 06,18:34 GMT)
Authors Keyword   balanced scorecard
  big data industry 4
  sustainable supply chain performance
Abstract In the era of Industry 4.0 and Big Data,sustainable supply chain is an emerging field od study.HOwever,the concept of balanced scoredcard approach assist is achieving performance goals,measurements, targets, and initiatives are translated from an organizatio's startegy.There are limited researchers found that incorporated the balanced socrecard approach to measure the sustainable supply chain.In order to evaluate the BSC concept and its effectiveness to susutainable supply chain this study was aimed to empirically examine the big data industry 4.0 influencing sustainable supply chain with and without balance scorecard. A quantitative approach used with convenient sampling. Data of 206 employees used to assess the hypothetical model.SmartPLS 4.0 software used to evaluate the study model. As a result, a significant relationship has noted between big data industry 4.0 and sustainable supply chain using a balanced scorecard approach.
